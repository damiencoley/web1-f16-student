“Maris Visions is about photography on a smaller scale making a big impact. For instance a small detail, most of us dont take the time to look at is aesthetically appreciated by this artist named Damaris Calderon. She implements a more colorful and vibrant effect on her visual artwork to express the energy to let people feel how she feels about the object or enviornment at that moment. Looking at most Boston photographers, she claims she has a different, but more of a raw perspective on what the city of Boston really means to her. She feels that shes here to express to you how little things also matter that make up a whole in any enviorment.”

— Maris Visions Instagram Page
